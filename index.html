Death Log

  • The human whose name is written in this note shall die.
  • This note will not take effect unless the writer has the person's face in their mind when writing his/her name.
  • If the cause of death is not specified, the person will simply die of a heart attack 40 seconds after their name is written.
  • If the cause of death is written within 40 seconds of the victim's name, that cause will be enacted.
  • Once the cause of death is written, the user has 6 minutes and 40 seconds (400 seconds) to detail the circumstances of the death.
